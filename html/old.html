  • What We Do

    WeListen convenes small-group discussions to humanize the other side

    The Problem

    America is divided

    Red states and blue states, echo chambers on social media, and a divisive presidential election. Americans can't seem to get along.

    Our Solution

    WeListen gets people talking

    We're fostering small-group discussion between unlikely conversants to change the American political climate—campus by campus.

  • About WeListen

    WeListen is a grassroots, campus-based organization bridging the American political divide.

    We're bringing together small groups of people with divergent political views to help them learn what others value and believe, using techniques based on neuroscience and political psychology.

    Our aim is to build a movement of listeners across the country willing to change the American political climate.
